Global Climate Crisis

 As the founder of Global Climate Crisis, I am dedicated to tackling climate change through environmental protection and sustainability. My activism stems from a deep concern for the planet and a desire to make a real impact.

One of our key initiatives is organizing beach cleanups, where volunteers unite to remove large amounts of trash, fostering a strong sense of community. We also engage with nursing homes and schools, delivering talks to raise awareness about climate change and encourage action.

In elementary schools, we conduct campus cleanups to teach young students the importance of environmental stewardship. We focus on practical steps to reduce carbon footprints, such as promoting vegetarian diets and advocating for electric vehicles and reduced air travel. Through workshops and resources, we empower individuals to make more sustainable choices in their daily lives.
